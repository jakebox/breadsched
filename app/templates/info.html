    Time to create the dough.

    First bulk rise.

  • 1st rise range

    Allowable over/under for the first bulk rise (how many minutes longer or shorter the rise can be).

    Second (shorter) rise.

    An action in between the two rises (ex: kneading, braiding).

    Time to bake the bread.

  • Cool time

    Time to cool the bread before eating.
